Explore and Join Our Ministries!

Come Be MCC with us and participate in one of our ministries listed below!

Be Worshipful:

The worship planning team plans and makes sure morning worship flows smoothly and is meaningful to both congregants and those participating.

Be Serving:

The service team as helps make sure Sunday worship is comfortable for our visitors as well as our regular attenders. Greeters meet all who come through our doors. Ushers make sure our worship area is clean and set up along with bulletins, offering envelopes along with any and all items are set up for our worship. Hospitality sets out coffee, water and any food items are set in a matter that is pleasing. These teams work together welcoming and insuring that all who enter feel welcome.

Be Musical:

Music is a very important part of worship. The music ministry bring the music to worship making sure that it flows with the themes and brings the worship experience to that greater level.

Be Caring:

The congregational care team whenever they have the opportunity, they make themselves available to in need of a faith based family. The Congregational Care Ministry intentionally ministered to the whole person body, mind and soul. They minister to members of all ages and all life events.

Be Prayerful:

The prayer angels is a group of Angels that pray for all. Yours is for the asking they have a prayer for you. Get involved in this ministry if you feel you could be of use adding in your prayers.

Be Administrative:

We have an elected administrative board which leads the church administratively. They meet once month to facilitate the operation of the church.

If you would like to get involved in one of these fun minitries, please sign in at the link below and submit a request. If you don't know your log in information, please contact us or speak to one of our staff or board members on any sunday!
