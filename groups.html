Become a Groupie!!!

Get involved and join one of our groups below to have fun and hang out with like minded church people!

Congregational Care Team:

The congregational care team whenever they have the opportunity, they make themselves available to in need of a faith based family. The Congregational Care Ministry intentionally ministered to the whole person body, mind and soul. They minister to members of all ages and all life events.

Crafting Group:

Join this group to have fun making awesome crafts with fellow church members.

Music Ministry:

Music is a very important part of worship. The music ministry bring the music to worship making sure that it flows with the themes and brings the worship experience to that greater level.

Spirit Watchers:

This group watches spritural movies every 3rd Wednesday of the month. Come join us as we experience Spiritual movies that then later leads to various spiritual discussions. There is usually a dinner provided where people may bring a beverage to share. People may also just come an watch the movie, which usually is at 7:30pm.

If you would like to get involved in one of these fun groups, please sign in at the link below and submit a request. If you don't know your log in information, please contact us or speak to one of our staff or board members on any sunday! To form a social/spiritual group fill out the groupie-application-form and submit it to the Mission and Marketing Team, via the church email address.
